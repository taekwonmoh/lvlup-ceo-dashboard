# Brand Content Structure

Every piece of content ladders back to one of four pillars. Tag each post to a pillar before it goes out so the overall mix stays balanced.

## Pillar 1 — Student Wins
- Stripe promotions
- Competition results
- First class stories
- Confidence transformations

## Pillar 2 — Family Culture
- Parent testimonials
- Birthday parties
- Community events
- Summer camp moments

## Pillar 3 — Instructor Expertise
- Technique breakdowns
- Coaching philosophy
- Training tips

## Pillar 4 — LVLUP Values
- Discipline
- Confidence
- Respect
- Leadership

## Additional Posts (mostly static)
- New Student Welcome
- Birthday Posts
- Competition Results
- Event Announcements

> Monthly content planning, along with the use of brand kits and templates, will help streamline the content creation process and speed things up.
