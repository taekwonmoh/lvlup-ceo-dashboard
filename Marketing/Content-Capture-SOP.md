# Weekly Content Capture Checklist (For Instructors)

## Minimum Requirement
Capture and upload by the end of the week:
- [ ] At least 3 minutes of raw video footage
- [ ] At least 3 quality photos

Upload all content to the designated shared drive.

## Before Recording
- [ ] Clean camera lens
- [ ] Record videos vertically (portrait mode)
- [ ] Ensure good lighting
- [ ] Keep the camera steady
- [ ] Move closer to the action

## Capture These Moments
- Student achievement (stripes, belts, competitions, first class, etc.)
- Student smiling during class
- Leadership moment
- Coach–student interaction
- Community or family moment
- B-roll (drills, warmups, students at attention, high fives, facility shots)

## Content Guidelines
- Video clips: 5–15 seconds
- Capture authentic moments
- Avoid excessive zooming
- Avoid filming from too far away
- Subjects should be clearly visible and in focus
- For martial arts poses, capture the full body whenever possible
- Avoid cutting off hands, feet, belts, or equipment

## Before Uploading
- [ ] 3+ minutes of video captured
- [ ] 3+ photos captured
- [ ] Files uploaded to the shared drive
- [ ] Student names and event details included (if applicable)
